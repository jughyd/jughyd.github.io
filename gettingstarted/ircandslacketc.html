Slack

Slack is a platform for team communication: everything in one place, instantly searchable, available wherever you go. To Use Slack goto JUG Hyderabad Slack Site You need to be invited to the group. Once you get an invitation, join the group and start communicating with others. You can subscribe to the individual channels to get latest updates of the respective channel. You generally subscribe to the channels you are interested in.
